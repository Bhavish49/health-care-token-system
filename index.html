Our Services

Everything a Modern
Hospital Needs

MediQ provides hospitals with a fully integrated digital token management ecosystem. At its core, the system eliminates physical token counters and replaces them with intelligent, cloud-backed queuing technology. Each patient receives a unique digital token upon arrival — either via the patient portal or directly at a facility kiosk — and is automatically notified in real time as their turn approaches, reducing waiting room congestion by up to 70%.

Beyond token issuance, MediQ delivers a comprehensive suite of tools for hospital administrators and clinical staff. Admins can onboard multiple doctors under a single facility, assign room numbers, define specializations, and monitor live queue metrics from a unified command dashboard. Doctors receive a dedicated clinical view to call, skip, or complete patient appointments with one click, while automated WhatsApp notifications keep patients informed at every step — turning a traditionally stressful experience into a seamless, dignified one.

Digital Token Issuance

Patients generate tokens instantly via the web portal. No counter, no paper, no confusion — just a unique queue number delivered to their phone.

WhatsApp Notifications

Automatic real-time alerts are sent to patients via WhatsApp the moment their turn is approaching, keeping them informed from anywhere.

Doctor Dashboard

Each doctor gets a personal live queue view to manage their patients efficiently — call next, mark complete, or skip with a single action.

Hospital Administration

Register and manage your entire hospital — multiple departments, multiple doctors, room assignments, and facility metrics in one place.

Multi-Doctor Support

Run parallel queues for every specialist under one hospital node. Cardiology, Neurology, Dermatology — all independent, all live.

Secure Access Control

Doctors verify themselves using hospital credentials before creating an account, ensuring only authorized medical staff join your facility.
